My Story

'It's not about what you acquire but what you experience, the journey that leads to an impactful life.'

I am a junior web and mobile developer specializing in React/React Native and various other technologies. I’m passionate about learning new technologies, I think it’s exciting to utilize my skillset to program an idea to life in a team environment for a fortune 500 company or for a small business owner’s customer experience that will leave a lasting impact online.

Starting at a young age I was always interested in learning about entrepreneurs and how they use their creative minds to solve problems through technological innovations. During my college career, I studied Economics at Minnesota State University and completed my Masters in Business at SNHU. My drive for success continued with Wells Fargo in customer relations and with Comcast as an Account Executive, and most recently as a Reverse logistics account manager with C.H. Robinson.

I value education, I work hard and strive to live a purposeful life with integrity that exceeds my own expectations. My drive for connecting and building strong client relationships derived from my entrepreneurial spirit in solving problems by co-founding Murhero Foundation, a nonprofit where I volunteer my free time to help inspire and motivate students and teachers in Sierra Leone. A few words that I live by as a programmer is the economic trade-off theory,

“One half of knowing what you want is knowing what you must give up before you get it” —Sidney Howard.
This quote identifies with the theory in such that we all have choices, for instance, when we choose to uplift others and put positive behaviors out into the world, we give up and eliminate any negative behaviors for a better everyday interactional experiences.
